About the Inventor

I'm Connor Owen—D1 soccer player at Columbia and former San Jose Earthquakes Academy player.

From club to college, I've seen it everywhere: players don't scan enough, and coaches don't have a reliable way to teach it.

I was lucky. My coaches emphasized scanning early on— but it still took constant yelling, and the habit didn't always stick.

That's why I created ScanBand: a coach on your head that quietly reminds you to scan— until it becomes instinct.
