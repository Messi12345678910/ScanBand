Get Yours

Simple.

ScanBand has two parts:

  • A rechargeable vibrating pod
  • A high-quality, washable headband

The headband's stitched-in compartment holds the pod, which slides out easily for charging.

The pod knows when your head turns.

When you're scanning like Messi, it's happy; when you're not, it vibrates to remind you.

Why I Created ScanBand.

I'm Connor Owen, a 17-year-old D1 soccer player for Columbia and former San Jose Earthquakes Academy (MLS Next) player. I've always loved two things: creating and soccer.
